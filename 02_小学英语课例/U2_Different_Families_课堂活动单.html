My Family Card

Name: \_\_\_\_ Class: \_\_\_\_

Part 1: Look and Match

Match the words with the pictures.

father        mother        brother        sister        family
Picture Word
1 \_\_
2 \_\_
3 \_\_
4 \_\_
5 \_\_

Part 2: Fill in the Blanks

Use the words to complete the sentences.

father / mother / brother / sister / family
  1. This is my \_\_.
  2. This is my \_\_.
  3. I love my \_\_.

Part 3: My Family Card

Draw or paste a picture of one family member.

This is my __________.

He / She is __________.

I love my family.

Part 4: Pair Work

Ask and answer with your partner.

A: Who is this?
B: This is my __________.

A: Is he/she nice?
B: Yes. He/She is __________.

Teacher Feedback

Optional Challenge
