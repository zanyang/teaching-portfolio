U3 Our Animal Friends 课堂活动单

Our Animal Friend

Name: \_\_\_\_ Class: \_\_\_\_

Part 1: Listen and Circle

Listen to the teacher and circle the animal.

cat     dog     bird     rabbit     fish

Part 2: Look and Say

Use the words to describe the animals.

big / small / cute / white / black / fast
  1. It is a \_\_.
  2. It is \_\_.
  3. It can \_\_.

Part 3: Ask and Answer

Work with your partner.

A: What is it?
B: It is a __________.

A: What color is it?
B: It is __________.

A: What can it do?
B: It can __________.

Part 4: Group Task

Draw your animal friend and introduce it.

Our animal friend is a __________.
It is __________.
It can __________.
We like it because __________.

Thinking Questions

  1. Are cats and dogs the same?
  2. What is different?
  3. Why do you like your animal friend?
